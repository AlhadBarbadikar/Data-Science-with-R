Applied Data Science with R

This repo is a master repository for STAT W4249 Applied Data Science offered at Columbia University. Spring 2016 is the first semester I am teaching. I am hoping to save all examples, lecture materials and students projects here for future reference and teaching. This is a new crowdsourcing way of teaching and learning.
